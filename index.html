Privacy PolicyTerms of Use

This Privacy Policy explains how the Tribal Herbal Remedies app ("the App", provided by inParlor Technologies Pvt Ltd) handles information. Contact: contact@inparlor.com. By using the App, you agree to this Policy.

Summary (plain language)
  • The App works offline and needs no account or login.
  • We don't collect your name, email, or location, and we have no servers storing your data.
  • Your in-app data (saved remedies/recipes, coins, settings, unlock status) stays on your device.
  • The free version shows ads via Google AdMob, which may use a device advertising identifier.
  • Purchases use Apple/Google's systems; we never see your payment details.
  • Ingredient links open Amazon through an affiliate program.

1. Information we do NOT collect

The App has no sign-up, login, or account. We do not ask for or collect your name, email, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, or health records, and we run no server that receives your personal data.

2. Information stored on your device

Saved/bookmarked remedies and recipes, "coins," daily free-view counts, your one-time unlock status, and app settings are stored locally on your device and are not sent to us. You can erase all of it by deleting the App.

3. Information collected automatically (advertising)

The free version displays ads using Google AdMob (with Google's User Messaging Platform for consent). To provide, personalize, cap, and measure ads, Google and its partners may collect: a device advertising identifier (Apple IDFA / Google Advertising ID), coarse location from IP address, and device/ad-interaction data. This is processed by Google as a third party under Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses data from apps that use its services. On iOS, the App shows Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt before any such identifier is used; declining still lets you use the whole App. We do not receive this advertising data in personally identifiable form.

4. In-app purchases

The optional one-time "unlock everything" purchase is processed entirely by the Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not receive or store your card or billing details — only a confirmation that a purchase or restore succeeded.

5. Affiliate links

Some screens link to buy ingredients on Amazon. These are affiliate links; if you purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. When you tap a link you leave the App and Amazon's privacy policy applies.

6. Notifications

If you enable reminders, the App schedules local notifications on your device. They are generated on-device and not sent through any server of ours. Turn them off anytime in your device settings.

7. Children's privacy

The App is intended for a general (adult) audience and is not directed to children under 13 (or your jurisdiction's minimum age). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your choices and rights

  • iOS – tracking: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, plus the in-app prompt.
  • Android – ads: Settings → Google → Ads (reset/delete advertising ID, opt out of personalization).
  • Ad consent (EEA/UK): the App shows a Google consent message where required.
  • Remove all local data: delete the App.
  • Under laws such as GDPR (EEA/UK) and CCPA/CPRA (California) you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data and opt out of certain uses. As we hold no personal data ourselves, advertising-data requests should go to Google; you may also contact us.

9. Data retention

We retain no personal data on our own systems. On-device data persists until you delete it; advertising data is retained by Google per Google's policies.

10. Security

On-device data is protected by your device's standard protections. Ad and purchase data in transit is handled over encrypted (HTTPS) connections by Google and Apple.

11. International users

Third-party providers (Google, Apple, Amazon) may process data in countries other than yours under their own safeguards.

12. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy; material changes will be reflected by an updated "Effective date" and posted at this URL.

13. Medical disclaimer

The App provides traditional and educational information only and is not medical advice. Never forage or consume a plant based on the App alone; consult a professional.
